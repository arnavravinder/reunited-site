Reunited Platform Guide

Lost something? Found something? You're in the right place. This guide walks you through everything you need to know about using Reunited to get your stuff back or help others find theirs.

What's Reunited All About?

Think of Reunited as your personal lost and found superhero. We've built this platform because losing stuff sucks, and finding it shouldn't be a nightmare. Whether you left your phone in the library or found someone's wallet in the cafeteria, Reunited makes the whole process actually work.

Actually Smart Search

No more endless scrolling. Our AI helps match your search with what's actually been found, so you spend less time looking and more time getting your stuff back.

Easy Login (No Hassle)

Sign in with Google, X, or just get a magic link sent to your email. We've got options because nobody likes password drama.

Track Everything

Know exactly where your claim stands. No more wondering if your request got lost in the void - you'll see updates in real time.

Keep It Real

Everyone can see what's been claimed (without personal details, obviously). It's all about trust and making sure the system actually works for everyone.

Getting Your Stuff Back

Found your item in the system? Awesome! Now comes the part where you prove it's actually yours and get it back. Don't worry - we've made this as straightforward as possible.

1. Spot Your Stuff

Search through what's been found. When you see your item, click on it to get started with your claim.

2. Prove It's Yours

Tell us something only you would know about the item. Like that tiny scratch you made or what's in the case.

3. Get Your Code

Once approved, you'll get a unique claim code. Think of it as your golden ticket to get your stuff back.

4. Pick It Up

Head to the Lost & Found office, show your code, and boom - you're reunited with your belongings!
