Welcome to the Reunited User Guide

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about finding your lost items and helping others reunite with theirs. Let's get you back together with your belongings!

Step 1: Search First! (The Most Important Step)

Before reporting a lost item, always search our database first! This is the fastest way to get your item back. Many items are found and logged before students even realize they're missing.

Browsing Found Items

When you search, you'll see item cards showing photos, descriptions, and where items were found. Items marked as "Claimed" have already been returned to their owners.

Step 2: Can't Find It? Report It.

If you can't find your item in the search results, don't worry! Report it as lost and our AI matching system will work in the background. When someone finds and logs an item that matches your description, you'll be notified automatically.

=ï¿½ Pro tip: The more detailed your description, the better our AI can match your item when it's found!

How to Claim an Item

Found your item? Great! Click on any item card to open a detailed view where you can see a larger photo and full description. If you're confident it's yours, hit the "Claim This Item" button.

What happens next? After claiming, you'll receive instructions on where and when to pick up your item. You may need to verify ownership by describing additional details about the item.

You're All Set!

That's everything you need to know about using Reunited! Remember: search first, report if needed, and claim when you find your items. We're here to help bring you and your belongings back together.
